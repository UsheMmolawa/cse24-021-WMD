About Warner Bros.

Warner Bros. is a globally recognized entertainment company, known for producing some of the most iconic films and TV shows in history.

Founded in 1923, Warner Bros. has brought to life legendary characters, unforgettable stories, and groundbreaking entertainment experiences.

With a commitment to creativity and innovation, Warner Bros. continues to be a leader in the entertainment industry, shaping the future of film and television.
